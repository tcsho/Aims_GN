The City School

-

Date: December 24th, 2020

Dear Parents,

We applaud you in trying to keep your child safe and fully understand that the COVID-19 pandemic has left you concerned about sending your child to school. At the same time, we appreciate that you may be facing issues managing devices for online classes at home. However, as educators we must encourage you to find a balance and maintain regular academic learning for your child during these challenging times.

Our records show that your child has not been physically attending school or our online classes during the 1st term of this academic year. Hence, it will not be possible to provide a report card which would normally show your child’s progress during the first term. We look forward to improved attendance during the 2nd term.

We appreciate your understanding in supporting the academic pathway of your child. Please contact the school regarding any support you may need.

Warmest regards

School Management

The City School Management
